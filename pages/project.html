Motion-powered Gameboy

Motion-powered Gameboy

ABSTRACT

We present ViPSN-Epaper, the first robust, motion-powered, user-friendly personal mobile gaming device. Our design is based on the ViPSN platform, implements with bistable energy harvester and bistable display. Bistable display technology makes it possible for screen-focused interactive battery-free devices to show image continuously even after power failure. Given the unique advantage of mechanical potential energy pre-charging, bistable energy harverster can be treat as a relatively stable energy source. With pre-designed bistable energy harvester and advanced energy management method, the amount of energy generated by user motion can properly meet with which ViPSN-Epaper needs to perform a reaction. The design methodology of ViPSN-Epaper provides a valuable aspect for the development of interactive motion-powered devices.
